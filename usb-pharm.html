Юзб Фарм (Usb Pharm)

from wiki - the free encyclopedia

A technological and pharmaceutical company that produces usb medicines for personal computers and software for their prevention. Its most well-known products are the line of usb tablets heal.me, the software reboot.me, and computer festival юзбфарм. It is the only company in the field of technological usb medicines.

The functioning of the organization is under the direction of AI Vladimir. This is an artificial intelligence of Soviet Developement. This technology was found in an abandoned laboratory in Siberia.

The ЮЗБФАРМ company helps to save a laptop computer at the right moment with its unique medicines and teaches people different computer skills.

A computer festival was created under the leadership of ЮЗБФАРМ. This festival immerses all visitors in computer life. The building where the festival is held is an analogue of Alcatraz Prison.

The first floor was created for enlightenment in the computer world. The second floor serves as a point for combating caught computer viruses, which are imprisoned on the 3rd floor. The third floor itself is a kind of oceanarium, but instead of marine life, the most ferocious viruses in the history of the Internet are collected there.
